Cover Letter

Dear Editor,

We are pleased to submit our manuscript entitled "Regional growth, convergence, and spatial spillovers in India: A reproducible view from outer space" for consideration in REGION.

Using satellite nighttime light data as a proxy for economic activity, this paper extends the influential study of Chanda and Kabiraj (2020, World Development) on regional convergence across 520 districts in India. Our work offers three main contributions that we believe will appeal to the journal's readership:

1. Interactive satellite imagery visualization. We develop an interactive tool that allows researchers to explore spatial and temporal patterns of regional convergence directly from satellite data, making growth hotspots and converging regions immediately visible.

2. Formal assessment of spatial dependence. We test for spatial autocorrelation in both the dependent and independent variables of the convergence equation, demonstrating that district-level economic trajectories are not independent of their neighbors.

3. Spatial spillover modeling. Using a spatial Durbin model, we show that spatial spillovers substantially increase the estimated speed of regional convergence beyond what conventional non-spatial estimates suggest. This finding carries policy implications: place-based interventions may generate benefits that extend beyond target districts through spatial multiplier effects.

A distinctive feature of this submission is its fully reproducible, open-science design. The manuscript is built using Quarto's single-source publishing system with Jupyter notebooks in Python, R, and Stata, ensuring that every analytical step is transparent and verifiable. An interactive HTML version of the manuscript, including all embedded visualizations and computational notebooks, is available at:

https://quarcs-lab.github.io/project2025s-py

We believe this work fits well within the scope of REGION given its focus on spatial analysis, regional convergence, and reproducible methodology.

Thank you for your consideration. We look forward to your response.
